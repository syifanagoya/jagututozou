Roskamala Debuts Dark Pop Anthem &quot;Kiss Me Amnesia&quot;

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Roskamala is a fascinating new artist with a knack for storytelling and musical composition. Her debut original single is called “Kiss Me Amnesia” and is released alongside a cinematic music video.

“Kiss Me Amnesia” is a riveting song all about unhealed trauma and how it can sabotage our happiness and growth. Roskamala paints the picture of a man who has dealt with a lot of past relationships that have left him hurting and scarred. Instead of confronting the pain, he retreats to an amnesia world full of past lovers. Roskamala confides, “the message is that if you don’t make peace with your past it will always haunt your future reality.” Roskamala is a true visionary and her creative prowess is shown with “Kiss Me Amnesia’s” intriguing lyrics and catchy melodies. This dark pop song is the first single off of her upcoming EP truth she told out next year.

Roskamala grew up in Thailand and moved to the United States to pursue her musical career. A singer, songwriter, producer, screenwriter and actor, the multi-talented woman has teamed up with acclaimed Grammy award-winning producers Ghian Wright (Paul McCartney, Janet Jackson) and Robert L. Smith (Lady Gaga, David Bowie) for her debut EP.
