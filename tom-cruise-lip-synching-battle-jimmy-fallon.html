Tom Cruise takes part in hilarious lip-synching battle

April 2024 · 2 minute read

    Tom Cruise is a man of many talents. The Hollywood superstar showed he is a force to be reckoned with as he took part in a lip-synching battle with Jimmy Fallon on Monday's Show. Tom, 53, gamely stepped up to the microphone to lip-sync two songs – and he certainly impressed.

    "This is so ridiculous. I love it," Tom laughed before miming along to his first song, by The Weeknd to the delight of the audience. It was then host Jimmy's turn, and he countered with a cover of the Rolling Stones classic , imitating Mick Jagger's trademark strut and singing style.
