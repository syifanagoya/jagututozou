August 2024 · 5 minute read

That Youtub3 family ages are David (44), Katie(44), Audrey Williams (23), Jordan Mae Williams(21), Jake(18), and Tyler Williams (14) years. The couple has four children.

That Youtub3 family is a group of six family members YouTube channel who vlog many recreational and challenging videos.

David is the father, and Katie is the mother of four children, two sons and two daughters.

The six members of the family joined ‘That Youtub3 Family’, a YouTube channel, on May 24, 2015, which is based on the family videos genre.

As stated in one of the videos, it was Jordan’s idea to start making videos as a family. One of her friends inspired Jordan to take this step.

However, before starting YouTube, the girls were keen videographers. They made videos on their camcorders.

The channel has about 3.61 million subscribers and sixteen hundred videos. Apart from this main channel, the kids have their own YouTube channels.

Audrey and Jordan have different channels, whereas Jake and Tyler run the same YouTube channel.
