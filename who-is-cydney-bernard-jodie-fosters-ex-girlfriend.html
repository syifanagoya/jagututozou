Cydney Bernard Worked as a Production Manager

Today, everyone knows Cydney Bernard as Jodie Foster’s ex-lover. Whenever her name is called, it is associated with Foster. According to our findings, Cydney Bernard has been active in Hollywood and has been working in production since the late 80s. In fact, in 1989, she worked as a production coordinator on Cold Feet, a comedy/Indie film. She also worked as a production coordinator on the 1990 comedy/fantasy film A Mom for Christmas, the 1991 Comedy/Romance film, L.A. Story, and the 1993 drama/romance movie Sommersby.

Bernard quickly rose from the position of production coordinator to production supervisor then to production manager, and eventually to producer. In 1993, she worked as the assistant production manager on the movie Family Prayers. She then served as the production supervisor on the 1994 thriller/drama film The Client. The same year, she worked yet again as the production supervisor on the sci-fi film Roswell.

In 1996, she became a production manager and worked in that capacity on the fantasy/adventure movie, Loch Ness. The same year, she served as the unit production manager on the western/drama movie, Ruby Jean and Joe before working as a unit production manager on the popular comedy/slapstick movie, Bean.

Bernard eventually became a producer. In 2000, she produced the fantasy/comedy, Ratz. The next year in 2001, she produced A Girl Thing, a made-for-television drama. In 2003, she produced the comedy/dramedy film, Spinning Boris. She also worked as a unit production manager on the TV series, The L Word in 2005. In 2011, she served as the unit production manager on the TV series, Off the Map.

Apparently, Cydney Bernard has paid her dues in Hollywood and is respected in some quarters for her work in the production department for many movies and series over the years.
