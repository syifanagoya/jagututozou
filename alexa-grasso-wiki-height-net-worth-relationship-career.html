How did Alexa Grasso start her Professional Career?

Alexa Grasso started her career as a mixed martial artist, and she took part in her first MMA battle in Mexico in December 2012. She participated in 5 fights during her MMA career, winning each one of them without suffering a setback.

Thereafter, Alexa focused on Invicta FC, winning her first match on September 6, 2014, in Invicta FC 8. Ashley Cummins was her opponent, and she won. She defeated Alida Gray in her second battle at Invicta FC 10 on December 5, 2014. She met Mizuki Inoue in February of the following year and defeated her as well, receiving her first Fight of the Night Bonus Award. After suffering a serious injury, she took some time off and avoided numerous gaming obstacles.

Similarly to this, Alexa committed to the UFC in August 2016. She defeated Heather Jo Clark in her debut UFC contest on November 5, 2016, and she won. She fought in UFC Fight Night 104 on February 4, 2014, but she lost the match to Felice Herrig.
