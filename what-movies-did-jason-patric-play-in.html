What movies did Jason Patric play in?

May 2024 · 1 minute read

Award-winning actor Jason Patric has appeared in many films including Solarbabies, The Lost Boys, After Dark, The Beast, Geronimo: An American Legend, Rush, Narc, The Girl in the Bathtub, Burning at Both Ends, Nightshade, Mk Ultra, and Becoming.

Jason Patric Career

Jason Patric has had a diverse acting career in film, television, and theater.

Patric comes from a family with a background in acting, as his father is the actor and playwright Jason Miller.

Patric began his acting career in the 1980s with roles in films like “The Lost Boys” (1987), which became a cult classic, and “The Beast” (1988).

He gained recognition for his performances in films like “Rush” (1991), where he starred alongside Jennifer Jason Leigh, and “Narc” (2002), a crime thriller. He also appeared in “Sleepers” (1996), “The Alamo” (2004), and “In the Valley of Elah” (2007).

Patric has also performed in theater productions, including the Broadway play “That Championship Season,” for which he received critical acclaim.

He has made occasional appearances in television series, such as “Wayward Pines” (2015), and “Powers” (2015-2016).

Patric has been in the public eye for various personal and legal issues, including a custody battle over his son and his involvement in assisted reproductive technology debates.
